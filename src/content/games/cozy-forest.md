## About

**Cozy Forest** is a touch-friendly virtual pet built for mobile and WebGL. Tend **Sprig**, a luminescent forest fawn, in a painterly clearing at dusk.

Tap world hotspots — berry bush, play stump, moss nest, trail arch — to care for hunger, energy, mood, and curiosity. Watch Sprig wander on their own, grow from Sprout to Guardian, unlock the moonlit glade, and collect handwritten diary lines between short check-ins.

Designed for guilt-free sessions: no death, no nagging, just gentle wonder in the woods.
